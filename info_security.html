Information Security Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the Information Security Policy of DiscAI. This policy outlines our commitment to safeguarding the security of information within our organization and protecting it from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Security Measures

We implement the following security measures to ensure the protection of information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption algorithms.
  • Access Controls: Access to systems and data is restricted based on the principle of least privilege.
  • Monitoring: We continuously monitor our systems for suspicious activities and potential security breaches.
  • Security Awareness: Regular training and awareness programs are conducted to educate employees about security best practices.
  • Incident Response: We have procedures in place to respond promptly to security incidents and mitigate potential damages.

Data Protection

We ensure the protection of data through:

  • Compliance: Adhering to applicable data protection laws and regulations.
  • Privacy Controls: Implementing privacy-enhancing technologies and policies.
  • Secure Development: Following secure coding practices in the development of our software.
  • Third-Party Risk Management: Assessing and managing the security risks posed by third-party vendors.

Responsibilities

It is the responsibility of every employee and contractor to:

  • Follow security policies and procedures.
  • Report any security incidents or vulnerabilities promptly.
  • Protect information assets entrusted to them.
  • Participate in security training and awareness programs.
