Data Protection Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the Data Protection Policy of DiscAI. This policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and manage your personal information. At DiscAI, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.

What Information Do We Collect?

We collect information that you provide to us directly when you interact with our services. This may include:

  • Personal information such as your name, email address, and Discord user ID.
  • Usage data related to your interactions with DiscAI, including commands used and server activity.
  • Technical information such as IP addresses, browser type, and device identifiers.

We may also collect information from third-party sources, such as Discord, to provide our services effectively.

How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services, including AI-powered functionalities and interactive features.
  • Improve and optimize our services, ensuring they meet your needs and expectations.
  • Communicate with you, including responding to inquiries and providing support.
  • Enforce our policies and terms of service.
  • Protect the security and integrity of our services and users.

How Do We Protect Your Information?

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. These include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest.
  • Regular security assessments and audits.
  • Access controls to limit unauthorized access to your information.
  • Strict internal policies and procedures for data handling.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your data, subject to legal requirements.
  • Opt-out of certain data processing activities, where applicable.
  • Withdraw your consent for the processing of your information.

To exercise these rights or if you have any questions about our data protection practices, please contact us using the details provided below.
